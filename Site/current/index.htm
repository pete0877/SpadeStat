Introduction

You are in the big blind of a $5/$10 game with the 67. There's an upfront raise and four callers after. What should you do?

So many answers in poker rely on the knowledge of odds - pot odds, implied odds, reverse-implied odds. However, if all you are doing is playing by the odds, you probably are not maximizing your potential.

In casinos and poker rooms, it is of utmost importance to get a line on your opponents. Without a read, all you can do is play your cards - oftentimes with very limited information.

With online poker - you will have a harder time getting that line on your opponents. There are no facial ticks online, but does that mean that reading your opponents isn't a factor online? Of course not.

Through the use of stat-tracking tools, players are able to maximize their edge over their opponents. In addition, with a tool, you can easily review your play and try to find flaws in your own game.
