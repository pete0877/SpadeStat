Features

Following is a full list of features of the SpadeStat software:
  • Hand Imports for Ring games, Tournaments and Sit and Go’s from Pokerstars
  • Hand histories supported for Hold’em, Omaha, Omaha Hi/Lo, 7-Card Stud, and 7-Card Stud Hi/Lo. Limit, No Limit and Pot Limit games are all supported
  • Notes capability on each player, including a future enhancement for easy categorization
  • Up to 5 separate types of reports about statistics available at the click of a button and are available in real time.
  • 13 Different criteria that can aid in customizing the standard reports to produce exactly the data you are looking for.
  • Advanced report generation for expert users familiar with SQL query language.
  • Export reports to MS Excel for easy review of reports from beyond the SpadeStat interface.
Following are the report types currently supported:
  • Tournament results - organized by player to show who has performed the best.
  • Ring game summaries to see which limits have proven to be the best for you overall - see your results in dollars per hour or big bets per 100 hands.
  • Action summaries - see how often your opponents are seeing the flop in comparison to you. See how often they raise or attempt a steal of a blind.
  • Hand Combination reports allow you to see just how often you are playing each hand combination - and whether it has been profitable for you. For a change, see what knowledge you have about your opponents and identify leaks in their games for the next time you sit with them.
